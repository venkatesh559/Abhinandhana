NEWS PAPER / MAGAZINE ADVERTISEMENT

Despite the onslaught of online media,thousands of people still continue to read newspapers daily.The reading habit trend does not show any signs of decline and instead seems to be growing higher up.This is what makes newspaper/magazine an attractive choice for reaching the largest collection of people.A well-conceived powerful advertising campaign in regional newspapers with the right words and design layout has the power to target targeted geographic audience and influence them instantly.

TELEVISION / RADIO ADVERTISEMENT

The great and unique thing about Television & Radio advertising is that you can target an audience which isn’t actively looking for your product or service, but you still get to attract their attention. A creative television and radio advertisement can achieve great results, provided the message is up-to-date and consistent. Further they are very efficient for they rely heavily on the fact that the audience is actively looking for your product or service.

TRANSIT MEDIA ADVERTISEMENT

To reach out to everybody that matters , Transit advertising is the way to go . Described as placement of print ads on buses and other vehicles and in bus shelters and train stations , it has proved to be an effective advertising medium for reaching an audience of all ages , backgrounds and incomes . This way the advertisement reaches families and professionals in their vehicles , people shopping around or students walking around the campus and above all , tourists finding their way around the city.

BILLBOARD / HOARDING ADVERTISEMENT

Billboard Advertising is an excellent way to gain visibility for your product/service or business profile and attract new customers.What better way can there be than reaching your customers when they are out and about with targeted roadside billboard/hoarding advertising.Abhinandana Media Services provides high-class billboard/hoarding advertising services that are innovative,powerful and cost-effective,using well designed and perfectly placed advertising.This in turn will help popularize the utility value or benefits of your product,service and connect your business with new as well as existing customers.
