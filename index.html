OUR CLIENTS

NEWSPAPER / MAGAZINE ADVERTISEMENT

Despite the onslaught of online media, thousands of people still continue to read newspapers daily. The reading habit trend does not show any signs of decline and instead seems to be growing higher up. This is what makes newspaper and magazine an attractive choice for reaching the largest collection of people. A well-conceived powerful advertising campaign in regional newspapers with the right words and design layout has the power to target targeted geographic audience and influence them instantly. Further, print advertising offers a fast turnaround in a country like India and enables you to have the advertisement you want, in the section you want, within a few days. Abhinandana Media Services with its holistic approach and fully integrated capabilities, provides a range of quality newspaper / magazine advertisement solutions. Our ability to understand consumer behaviors can help you design and implement targeted advertisements for readers of popular newspapers.

TELEVISION / RADIO ADVERTISEMENT

The great and unique thing about Television & Radio advertising is that you can target an audience which isn’t actively looking for your product or service, but you still get to attract their attention. A creative television and radio advertisement can achieve great results, provided the message is up-to-date and consistent. Further they are very efficient for they rely heavily on the fact that the audience is actively looking for your product or service. We at Abhinandana offer result-oriented innovative television and radio advertisement solutions that have seen the best results for our clients with a focused effort. Whether you’re a nascent, unbranded start-up or an established organization looking to advertise your product / service in a television advertisement, then look to us. We can take your requirement and amplify it with your message to your targeted audience. The aim is to express the campaign intentions creatively and effectively, prompting customer’s reactions.

TRANSIT MEDIA ADVERTISEMENT

To reach out to everybody that matters, Transit advertising is the way to go. Described as placement of print ads on buses and other vehicles and in bus shelters and train stations, it has proved to be an effective advertising medium for reaching an audience of all ages, backgrounds and incomes. This way the advertisement reaches families and professionals in their vehicles, people shopping around or students walking around the campus and above all, tourists finding their way around the city. The greatest benefit of Transit Advertising is that you cannot ignore it nor can it be turned off like television. It reaches people driving and passengers no matter what they’re looking at or doing. With its large, colorful, innovative designs that demand attention, it offers flexibility of ad size and location while delivering a varied audience. Abhinandana Media Services provides a host of quality transit advertising services that will allow you to engage with audiences when they work, shop and play. Through creative and eye-catching advertisement design and content, our team will ensure large-scale opportunities for you to reach the right audience. If you plan on a transit advertisement, speak to us about placing an ad in a bus shelter or a moving bus or truck. We will ensure an advertisement that will use bright colors with simple ideas laced by a few words for effective advertising that will attract attention.

BILLBOARD / HOARDING ADVERTISEMENT

Billboard Advertising is an excellent way to gain visibility for your product / service or business profile and attract new customers. What better way can there be than reaching your customers when they are out and about with targeted roadside billboard / hoarding advertising. Abhinandana Media Services provides high-class billboard / hoarding advertising services that are innovative, powerful and cost-effective, using well designed and perfectly placed advertising. This in turn will help popularize the utility value or benefits of your product / service and connect your business with new as well as existing customers. If you’re looking to have a billboard / hoarding advertisement in a particular location, place or road, get in touch with our sales team who will then contact you to discuss your requirements.
